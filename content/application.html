--- title: Plot Application slug: application ---

The garden plots are behind the Emerald House property, 67 Davisville Road, East Falmouth. We hope to be able to support up to 30 gardeners. Most beds will be standard plots measuring 10’x10’ with no elevation above ground level. In addition we will have at least two raised beds for gardeners with limited mobility and at least one wheelchair accessible bed too.

Water will be available approximately every 20-30’ in the garden. The fence surrounding the garden will be 5’ high 1 inch Deerbuster hex fencing with 1’ buried for critter control. We will have at least two access gates, one of which will be wheelchair accessible.

Plots will be $25 for the season. A limited number of waivers are available - please email us (emeraldcommunitygarden@gmail.com) for more details. Applications are only open to Falmouth residents. A paper copy of the application form is available from the Falmouth Service Center.

The deadline for applying for a plot is March 21 at 11:59PM

If you have any questions, please contact us at emeraldcommunitygarden@gmail.com.
